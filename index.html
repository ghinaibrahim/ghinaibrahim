About

UX/UI & Product Designer

Hi, I’m Ghina! A curious creative person with a passion for solving problems through design. My journey started in coding and MIS, where I built a strong foundation in IT, Web developement and Wordpress.
During a life coaching training program, I discovered my true passion for design, understanding people and creating meaningful experiences.

This revelation drew me into the world of UI/UX, I realized I didn't just want to build functional tools; I wanted to build tools that felt intuitive and easy for people to use.
This led me to the SE Factory Bootcamp, where I expanded my technical and collaborative skills in UI design.

Today, I’m a Product Designer who loves taking a messy idea and turning it into something that actually makes sense for the person using it. I believe great design is about more than just looking good, it’s about making someone’s day a little easier through smart, thoughtful choices.
